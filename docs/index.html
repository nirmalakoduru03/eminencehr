Our Delivery Process

Structured, transparent, and outcomes‑focused from brief to onboarding.

1. Pre‑Assignment

Understand client’s business. Design solutions. Map skills, grade equivalence, targeted industry and market segment.

2. Search & Assessment

Portals and database search, social outreach, headhunting. Three‑stage filtering, competency and psychometric assessments (as mandated).

3. Selection & Pre‑Joining

Interview coordination, offer support, salary negotiation, reference checks (as required), and candidate orientation.

4. Post‑Joining

Continuous engagement for the first 60 days, surfacing challenges and feedback to the organization and candidate.
